Why choose Laundr?

restore

Get time back!

The average New Yorker wastes two hours per week doing their laundry. With Laundr, recapture the lost time that can be better spent enjoying life!

How much does it cost?

credit_card

Less than most laundromats!

The average load of laundry in NYC costs $3, which doesn't include fold, pickup, or dropoff services. By eliminating brick and mortar laundromats, we can save users serious dough!

How does it work?

group

We connect people that need clean laundry to washers

By creating a large network of people who are available now to get your laundry load done, we create an efficient and fast way to do away with life's most tedious work.

Created by New Yorkers, for New Yorkers

Initially created as a demo project, Laundr has become a New York phenomenon! We "blame" our fellow New Yorkers, who like us were sick of wasting their lives on time-comsuming chores. We all know laundry day is coming up, but Laundr is here to make it as easy as ordering a pizza!
